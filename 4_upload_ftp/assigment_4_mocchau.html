Admire the Beauty of Moc Chau Highland

MOC CHAU
NORTH EAST

Moc Chau plateau in Son La province, the Northeast Vietnam is the beautiful destination, about 200 km from Hanoi city. At the height of 1050m above sea level, Moc Chau plateau is well known with green tea hills, ethnic minority villages and the wild beauty of nature. The place has breathtaking taking nature in the sunset and sunrise.
